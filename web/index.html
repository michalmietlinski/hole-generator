Printing tips

These hole covers are designed for FDM printing and a snug press-fit into furniture holes.

  • Print in PETG or PLA with 0.2 mm layers for a good balance of detail and time.
  • Start with 2–3 wall lines and 20–40% infill for a solid plug.
  • If the fit is too tight or loose, adjust the fit tolerance in steps of 0.1–0.2 mm.
  • For cable pass-throughs, use the hollow mode and ensure cables can move freely without pinching.
